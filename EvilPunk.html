EvilPunk

Introduction to the project

EvilPunk was a group project in which I and 4 game artists made a game for a school project. There were originally meant to be 2 developers but my teammate who was assigned to our group didnt want to do this study anymore and right at the start left me to be the only developer. And since this was only the second game i ever made i struggled quite a lot with this project.

Gameplay

EvilPunk is a top down 2.5d shooter where you can use different guns to attack every increasing waves of enemies, which become harder the further down the waves you get.

Enemy Types

Walker

A basic slow enemy that attacks you when you are close to it

Slasher

A big enemy with tols of HP that can be really dangerous once it gets close to you

Bomber

A Fast enemy with low HP that explodes when it gets close enough to you

Enemy art by Felishaus Visit their ArtStation

Guns

Clockwork Pistol

A standard handgun with moderate damage and accuracy.

Steam Shotgun

A close-range weapon that fires a spread of bullets

Automatic Rifle

A fast-firing fully automatic rifle which is sure to shred your enemy

Arc Launcher

A weapon that shoots a ball of energy dealing massive damage

Weapon art by Nou Maaijen Visit their ArtStation

Gun Selector Functionality

The Gun Selector allows players to switch between different weapons during gameplay. This feature is essential to the game since you otherwise wouldnt be able to use the different guns in the game.
